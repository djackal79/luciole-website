# Pull Another One — Game Mechanics

## Turn Structure

1. **Draw a Performance Card** — The active player draws a randomly selected Performance Card from the box. The app displays the card image and a randomly assigned card name.

2. **Reveal the Prompt** — Tap **Show Prompt →** to reveal the challenge. Each prompt is tied to the card's venue (suit):
   - 🎤 Comedy Club
   - 🎵 The RSL
   - 🎪 Royal Show
   - 🎭 School Play

3. **Perform** — The active player attempts the prompt.

4. **Judge the Result**
   - **Unsuccessful** — The success condition was not met. Tap **✗ Fail**. The card is discarded and play passes automatically to the next player.
   - **Successful** — The success condition was met. Tap **✓ Pass**. The player then chooses:
     - **Keep Card** — Hold on to the Performance Card (worth points at end of game). Play passes to the next player.
     - **Gain Token** — Collect a token from the bank and discard the card. Play passes to the next player.

## Success Conditions

| Icon | Type | How to judge |
|------|------|-------------|
| 🗳️ | Majority vote | More than half the table agrees the prompt was completed |
| ✅ | Objective | The stated objective was clearly met |
| 👤 | Named judge | The player to the left decides |

## Cards

- Each player has a **Character Card** (their identity) and a numbered **Prompt Card** (1–16) which determines which prompts they draw from each venue.
- If all prompts at a venue are exhausted on a player's card, draw a bonus card (9–16) from the box.
- The app tracks used prompts per player per venue and shows venue progress on the main screen.

## Family Mode

Toggle Family Mode to hide adult-themed prompts. Safe for all ages when enabled.
